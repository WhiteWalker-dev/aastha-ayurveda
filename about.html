About Aastha Ayurveda

Celebrating over two decades of traditional healing, academic excellence, and international patient care since 2004.

Established 2004

Two Decades of Ayurvedic Wisdom

Aastha Ayurveda was founded in New Delhi with a clear mission: to provide authentic, text-based Ayurvedic healthcare in a modern clinic layout. Over the years, the clinic has developed from a local outpatient center into an internationally recognized hub for chronic disease management and Panchakarma training.

Our treatment system goes beyond symptom suppression. We focus on diagnosing metabolic root causes, clearing systemic toxins (Ama), and counseling patients on diet (Ahara) and lifestyle (Vihara) to restore long-term cellular balance.

Our Mission

To preserve and deliver the authentic teachings of Charaka and Sushruta Samhita, providing premium, safe, and custom healthcare options that integrate seamlessly with modern life.

Our Vision

To establish Aastha Ayurveda as a global center of excellence where ancient healing methods are validated, taught, and trusted by medical professionals and students worldwide.

Professional Biography

Dr. Sandeep Madaan is a renowned Ayurvedic Physician and Mentor based in New Delhi. Over the course of 20+ years, he has successfully diagnosed and treated more than 10,000 patients suffering from chronic lifestyle disorders, neurological issues, and arthritic problems.

He holds a Doctor of Medicine (MD) in Ayurveda, specializing in pharmacology (Dravyaguna) and Panchakarma therapies. As Academic Director of Aastha Academy, he has mentored hundreds of yoga teachers, medical doctors, and massage therapists from Australia, Germany, USA, UK, and India.
