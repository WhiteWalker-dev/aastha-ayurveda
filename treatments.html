01. GASTROINTESTINAL HEALTH

Digestive Disorders

Ayurveda views digestion (Agni) as the cornerstone of overall vitality. Imbalances in Agni lead to the formation of metabolic toxins (Ama), which triggers systemic inflammation. We treat chronic conditions like IBS, ulcerative colitis, hyperacidity, bloating, and chronic constipation through systematic digestion restoration.

Common Conditions Managed:

  • Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)
  • Gastroesophageal Reflux (GERD)

Panchakarma Detoxification

Panchakarma is the ultimate cleansing method in Ayurveda.
